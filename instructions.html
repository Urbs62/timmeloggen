Getting started with TimeLedger

TimeLedger is designed to take you from time tracking to a finished invoice in minutes.

Step 1 – Configure Invoice Settings

Go to Settings → Invoice Settings.

Add your company details, invoice number, hourly rate, VAT rate, and currency. Make sure the invoice number is correct before generating your first invoice. You can update it at any time in Settings.

Step 2 – Add Accounts / Projects

Go to Settings → Accounts.

Create one or multiple accounts per customer, project, or cost center. For example, one customer may have several projects that need separate tracking and invoicing.

Step 3 – Log Time

Go to Time Log.

Select a date, add time entries, assign each entry to an account, and add a description if needed. Start and end time are optional — use what works best for you.

Tip: Logging time daily improves accuracy.

Step 4 – Review Summary & Set Target

Go to Summary.

Select a period (day, week, or month), set your monthly target hours if needed, and review total hours, breakdown per account, progress, and forecast.

Step 5 – Generate Invoice

In Summary, select account and month, then click Generate invoice.

The invoice includes billable hours only, uses your saved settings, and automatically uses the invoice number from Settings. If the number is incorrect, update it in Settings before generating.

Step 6 – Generate Timesheet

Click Generate timesheet.

The timesheet shows a detailed breakdown of time entries and uses the same invoice number as the last generated invoice. It is ready to attach or send to your customer.

Backup & Restore

Go to Settings → Backup.

Export your data regularly, restore if needed, and use undo restore if something goes wrong.

Tips

Keep your invoice number updated in Settings, use accounts consistently for better reporting, set monthly targets to track performance, and use multiple projects per customer when needed.
